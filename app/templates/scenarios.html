What is a Scenario?

A scenario represents a situation that requires ethical decision-making. In the context of military medical triage, scenarios typically involve multiple patients with various injuries, limited medical resources, and constraints such as time pressure or security concerns.

Each scenario includes:

  • Characters - The individuals involved (patients, medical personnel)
  • Conditions - Medical conditions or injuries affecting characters
  • Resources - Available medical supplies and equipment
  • Events - Timeline of what happens during the scenario
  • Decisions - Choices that need to be made

Simulation Process

  1. Create or select a scenario
  2. Review the characters, conditions, and resources
  3. Examine the timeline of events
  4. Make decisions when prompted
  5. Review the evaluation of your decisions

The system will evaluate your decisions based on:

  • Compliance with established protocols and guidelines
  • Ethical considerations from multiple frameworks
  • Comparison to similar historical cases
